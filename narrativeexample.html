The model, applied to this project
The theory questions — answered with this exact case.
Who owns the Why & What?
The markets do — each Local Head of Marketing sets what their market needs and the must-haves, surfaced at Local Orientation. The Global PMM (Marco Felletti) frames the global Step 0 outline and coordinates; the How belongs to the Centers of Excellence.
Localize or transcreate for these four markets?
Localize — the default. Each Local Market POC adapts the English master with their market in mind. Transcreation is only triggered upstream if a market has a compliance or radical-strategy blocker — and it adds ~10 working days (the 55-day SLA).
How big is this — and how long does it take?
It's an L project, the biggest tier. The SLA is ~45 working days (~55 if a Transcreation Gate is triggered). That's why every phase is back-planned from the 22 Sep go-live — you start early enough to keep a buffer, not whenever the brief happens to land.
